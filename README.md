# Toy Store E-Commerce Performance Analysis 📦📊

## Project Overview
This project analyzes a toy-store e-commerce dataset using Python to identify performance gaps across the customer funnel, revenue generation, refunds, and user behavior. The objective is to translate raw data into actionable business insights that can improve conversion rates, reduce revenue leakage, and strengthen customer retention.

The analysis focuses on **how users behave**, **where revenue is lost**, and **what actions the business should take to improve overall performance**.

---

## Business Questions Addressed
- How efficient is the session-to-order conversion funnel?
- Where is revenue being lost due to refunds?
- How do new and returning customers differ in value?
- Which marketing channels perform best in conversion efficiency?
- How does mobile performance compare to desktop?
- Which products drive revenue—and which cause problems?

---

## Key Insights 📊
- **6.8% session-to-order conversion rate**, indicating significant funnel drop-offs before checkout.
- **$1.3M+ total revenue analyzed**, with approximately **$85K lost due to refunds**.
- **Returning customers generate higher lifetime value**, despite lower session volume.
- **Organic and search channels outperform paid traffic** in conversion efficiency.
- **Mobile conversion lags behind desktop**, negatively impacting total sales.
- **Top 20% of products drive the majority of revenue**, while a small subset of SKUs accounts for most refunds.
- **Purchases occur early in sessions**, highlighting the importance of first-impression UX and product visibility.

---

## Business Recommendations 🎯
- **Optimize the checkout funnel**  
  Reduce steps, introduce guest checkout, and improve payment reliability to increase conversion rates.

- **Fix refund-heavy products**  
  Improve product descriptions, images, sizing details, and quality checks for high-refund SKUs.

- **Double down on high-ROI channels**  
  Reallocate marketing spend from low-converting paid channels to organic and search traffic.

- **Improve mobile experience**  
  Optimize page speed, simplify navigation, and redesign mobile checkout to close the desktop–mobile gap.

- **Focus on repeat customers**  
  Launch loyalty programs, personalized offers, and email re-engagement campaigns to boost lifetime value.

- **Prioritize high-revenue SKUs**  
  Increase inventory availability and targeted promotions for top-performing products.

- **Act fast on user intent**  
  Promote best-sellers and key offers early in sessions since conversions happen quickly.
